What We Do

How does style transfer work?

Our website employs deep learning in order to perform its style transfer magic. For each image in the artwork gallery, we have created a convolutional neural network model. Each of these models were trained using the ImageNet dataset according to the method described in J. Johnson, et. al.'s 2016 CVPR paper, "Perceptual Losses for Real-Time Style Transfer and Super-Resolution." The basic goal of style transfer is to transfer the style of an image to another with as little style loss as possible. At the same time, contents of the image we copy style to should remain as unaltered as possible. Therefore, this task must also be accomplished with as little content loss as possible. In their paper, J. Johnson and his team devised a method in which this task can be accomplished magnitudes faster than previous approaches. For additional details, please see their paper here.
